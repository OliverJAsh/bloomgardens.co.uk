About

Bloom Horticulture is a professional domestic and commercial gardening company, based in Rushden, Northamptonshire and covering the surrounding areas.

Bloom Horticulture was set up by Alexander Ash, a professional gardener who has been a gardener for 11 years with experience serving commercial and domestic clients.

Services

Regular visits (weekly, fortnightly, or monthly) to make sure your garden is looking good all year round, domestic and commercial. We can help with:

  • Grass cutting
  • Hedge cutting
  • General maintenance
  • Edging
  • Leaf clearing
  • De-weeding
  • Pruning
  • Planting of herbious and evergreen borders
  • Lawn care
